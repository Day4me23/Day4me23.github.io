About Me

I am currently a student at the University of Wisconsin Whitewater achieving my Bachelor's degree in Media Arts and Game Development with a programming emphasis. I am also studying a minor in Web Development and Administration. Throughout my time at UW Whitewater, I have been a proud straight A student.

While progressing through my curriculum, I am actively searching for new ways to challenge myself through internal and external opportunities. Some of the activites I have participated in go as followed.

Media Arts and Game Development Tutor
-I work on campus as a lab monitor where I am able to tutor students in my major.
Motion Capture Studio
-Trained to use the campus MOCAP studio where I work with the Motive and MotionBuilder software.
Independent Study
-Working 1 on 1 with Professor Nick Hwang on multiple aspects of game development including Level Design, UI, and Objectives.
